Accessibility Statement

Date of Last Review: December 5, 2025

1. Commitment to Accessibility

MyLibCom is committed to providing a website that is accessible to the widest possible audience, regardless of technology or ability. We are actively working to increase the accessibility and usability of our website and in doing so adhere to many of the available standards and guidelines.

2. Conformance Status

The MyLibCom platform endeavors to conform with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA. WCAG guidelines define three levels of conformance: A, AA, and AAA. Our goal is Level AA conformance, which is widely recognized as providing a satisfactory level of accessibility for digital content.

3. Non-Accessible Content

While MyLibCom strives to adhere to the accepted guidelines and standards for accessibility and usability, it is not always possible to do so in all areas of the website. We are aware of some areas where we could improve accessibility:

  • Legacy Content: Some older documents or archived e-book previews may not be fully accessible or properly tagged for screen readers.
  • Third-Party Embeds: Content embedded from external sources (e.g., video players or third-party advertisements) may not fully conform to WCAG standards. We encourage users to contact the third party directly in these cases.
  • Dynamic Elements: Certain complex, interactive components, particularly those handling advanced search filters, may not be fully optimized for keyboard-only navigation.

4. Feedback and Contact

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of MyLibCom. Please let us know if you encounter accessibility barriers on the site. We try to respond to feedback within 5 business days.
